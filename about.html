About Royal Grand Hotel

Our Story

Established in 1985, Royal Grand Hotel has been a beacon of luxury and hospitality for over three decades. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a home away from home for travelers seeking comfort, elegance, and exceptional service.

Today, we stand as one of the most prestigious hotels in the region, known for our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and warm hospitality that makes every guest feel like royalty.

History & Heritage

Our magnificent building is a perfect blend of traditional architecture and modern amenities. The heritage structure was carefully restored to preserve its historical significance while incorporating contemporary luxury standards.

Over the years, we have hosted dignitaries, celebrities, and countless families, each leaving with cherished memories and stories to tell. Our walls echo with decades of laughter, celebrations, and joyous moments.
